## Interpretation

The prior $40,361.94 result from the 2012-2022 internal candidate fixture did not generalize to the independent 2016-2026 FXCM feed. The discrepancy indicates strategy-selection overfitting, source sensitivity, or both. It would be unsafe to merge or activate the frozen V14.8 profile.

The appropriate next research step is to retrain new candidate strategies on a strictly separated pre-2022 segment of the external FXCM data, freeze them, and evaluate them only once on the untouched March 2022-May 2026 segment. The failed V14.8 parameters must not be adjusted using that fresh segment and then reported as out-of-sample.

## Method limits

- H1 bid and ask candles came from FXCM's weekly archive.
- H4 and D1 candles were resampled from H1 bid candles.
- Retail costs were represented using the existing fixed R reserves and observed spread distributions.
- This is a bar-based replay, not tick-level broker execution with exact commission, swap and slippage schedules.
- Historical results do not guarantee future performance.
